    Quattro Formaggi

    A hot take on a four cheese favourite: Pecorino, Italian blue cheese, mozzarella and Gran Milano cheese with tomato and rosemary. Drizzled with smoky chilli jam on a Romana base.

    Padana

    Goat’s cheese, mozzarella, caramelised onion, spinach, red onion, tomato and garlic oil on a Romana base.

    Margherita

    Mozzarella and tomato on a Romana base. Vegan option available.

    Porchetta

    Porchetta; slow-cooked pulled pork is a traditional part of many Italian feasts. We’ve put it on a white béchamel base with crispy pancetta, mozzarella, garlic oil, fennel seeds and Gran Milano cheese.
